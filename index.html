Process

Maximize your digital marketing return on investment.

  • Strategy

    Strategy incorporates building a system inventory and defining the digital architecture of all web and social media properties and conducting a thorough SEO audit. This is followed by a developing of content and measurement strategies along with targeting appropriate goals and KPIs.

  • Implementation

    Implementation is the timely deployment of appropriate tactics which leverage strategies to meet objectives. This stage involves the development of a digital architecture and integrating data and content to deliver a great user experience. The process is supported by tag management, usability testing, and the creation of dashboards to automate reporting.

  • Optimization

    In the long lifetime of technology, no system can ever be static, and continuous improvement is necessary to maintain a competitive advantage. Optimization involves seeking new ways to elevate and innovate the system to keep it agile and responsive. Key optimization tools include personalization of websites and social media properties; search engine optimization; local and paid search management.

  • Analytics

    Analytics involves gaining valuable insights from the data to get clear and specific information that leads to better decision making, greater efficiency, and a competitive advantage. Analytics involves data management to harness complex and overwhelming amounts of data, business intelligence to develop new ways to extract knowledge and value from data, forecast and optimize business performance, and data visualization to tell the story behind the data.
